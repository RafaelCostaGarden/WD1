The basic layouts of webpages on a website can be sketched out using a wireframe, where various sized rectangles with black outlines are placed on a hypothetical space and labelled with their funtions, like image, text, logo, navigation, etc. To figure out how the various webpages of your website will interconnect, one can use a site map. A site map is when one places representations of each web page together, and draws lines based on how they interconect, as well as if a webpage is accessible from others and vice versa.
